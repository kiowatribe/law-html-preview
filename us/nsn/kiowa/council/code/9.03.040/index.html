9.03.040 Kiowa Tribe Housing Assistance

The Tribe requests Kiowa Tribe Housing Authority to continue on an annual basis henceforth to manage any funds if provided by the Kiowa Tribe for the two housing activities mentioned above.

(Kiowa Tribe Res. No. KL-CY-2020-004.)

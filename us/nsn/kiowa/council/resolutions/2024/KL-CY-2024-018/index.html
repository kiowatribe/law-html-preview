KL-CY-2024-018 - Repealing of Resolution KL-CY-2017-014 Employee Benefits Provider Selection Act of 2017.

At a duly called Session of the Legislature of the Kiowa Tribe held this 14th day of September, the following Resolution and Law were adopted.

WHEREAS; the Legislature is vested with the authority to pass laws and resolutions pursuant to Article VI, Section 6(a) of the Constitution of the Kiowa Tribe; and,

WHEREAS; the Kiowa Constitution, Article VI, Section 6(a) Laws and Resolutions which have been enacted shall remain valid until amended or repealed; and,

WHEREAS; the Legislature shall repeal the enacted Resolution KL-CY-2017-014 Employee Provider Selection Act of 2017, due to the formation of Articles of Incorporation for Kiowa Insurance, Inc. A Kiowa Tribe Corporation enacted through Resolution KL-CY-2023-011 Kiowa Tribe Uniform Commercial Business Codes of 2023; and,

WHEREAS; pursuant to Articles of Incorporation for "Kiowa Insurance Inc." is wholly owned by the Kiowa Tribe in accordance with Article 5 of KL-CY-2023-011 Kiowa Tribe Uniform Commercial Business Codes of 2023, Section 4.1, 1) to procure insurance and comprehensive employee benefit plans, including health and retirement, of all kinds for the Kiowa Tribe, including, but not limited to: the three branches of government and their respective employees; current and future property and business.

NOW THEREFORE IT BE IT RESOLVED; that the Legislature hereby repeals Resolution KL-CY-2017-014 Employee Provider Selection Act of 2017.

C E R T I F I C A T I O N

The foregoing Resolution KL-CY-2024-018 was duly voted upon by the Legislature on September 14, 2024, at a Legislative Regular Session LXXXIX-(89), with a vote of (6) in favor and (0) opposed, (0) abstaining, and (0) absent, according to the authority vested in the Legislature by the Constitution of the Kiowa Tribe.

Michael Primus

Speaker of the Legislature

KL-CY-2023-011 - Kiowa Tribe Uniform Commercial Business Code of 2023

At a duly called Session of the Legislature of the Kiowa Tribe held this 17th day of May, 2023, the following Resolution and Law were adopted.

WHEREAS; the Legislature is vested with the authority to pass laws and resolutions pursuant to Article VI, Section 6(a) of the Constitution of the Kiowa Tribe; and,

WHEREAS; the Legislature shall enact a uniform Commercial Business Code, for any lawful business purposes under the Kiowa Tribe; and,

WHEREAS; the Legislature has determined that nothing herein shall be construed as a waiver of the Kiowa Tribe sovereign immunity from suit.

NOW THEREFORE IT BE IT RESOLVED; that the Legislature hereby enacts the attached law entitled, "Kiowa Tribe Uniform Commercial Business Code of 2023".

CERTIFICATION

The foregoing Resolution KL-CY-2023-011, was duly voted upon by the Legislature on May 17, 2023, at a Legislative Special Session II-(2) with a vote of (5) in favor and (0) opposed, (1) abstaining, and (1) absent, pursuant to the authority vested in the Legislature by the Constitution of the Kiowa Tribe.

Anita Johnson

Secretary of the Legislature

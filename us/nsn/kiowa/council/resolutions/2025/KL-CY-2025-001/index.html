KL-CY-2025-001 - The Kiowa Tribe Recognizes and Honors Kiowa Veterans Who Have Received the Purple Heart Medal.

WHEREAS, the Kiowa Tribe is a federally recognized Indian Tribe with a Constitution approved by the Secretary of the Interior; and,

WHEREAS, the Legislature, is vested with the authority to pass laws and resolutions pursuant to Article VI, Section 6 (a) of the Constitution of the Kiowa Tribe; and,

WHEREAS, the Kiowa Tribe recognizes and honors Kiowa Tribe Veterans who have received the "Purple Heart Medal" while serving in the U.S. Military, for their volunteerism and selfless service in combat while defending our great Nation; and,

WHEREAS, this exceptional group of Kiowa Tribe Veterans, men and women paid the ultimate sacrifice, while in the line of duty for their outstanding contributions to our Nation's security; and,

WHEREAS, the Legislature designates August 07 as Kiowa Tribe Veterans Purple Heart Day.

NOW, THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, the Kiowa Tribe Legislature, hereby designates August 07 as Kiowa Tribe Veterans Purple Heart Day and calls on all Kiowa Tribe Citizens to join in honoring purple heart medal recipients who have served and those who will serve our Nation with distinction.

C E R T I F I C A T I O N

The foregoing Resolution KL-CY-2025-001 was duly voted upon by the Legislature on February 08, 2025, at a Legislative Regular Session XCIV-(94), with a vote of (7) in favor and (0) opposed, (0) abstaining, and (0) absent, according to the authority vested in the Legislature by the Constitution of the Kiowa Tribe.

Michael Primus

Speaker of the Legislature

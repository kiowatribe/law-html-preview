KL-CY-2025-005 - A Resolution Affirming Tribal Data Sovereignty, Establishing Protections Against Unauthorized Data Collection and Usage

At a duly called session of the Legislature of the Kiowa Tribe held this 12th day of April, the following Resolution and Law were adopted.

WHEREAS, the Legislature is vested with the authority to pass laws and resolutions pursuant to Article VI, Section 6(a) of the Constitution of the Kiowa Tribe; and,

WHEREAS, since time immemorial, the Kiowa Tribe has maintained sovereign governance over its people, lands, knowledge, and resources, including all forms of information that pertain to its members, culture, traditions, and territories; and

WHEREAS, the inherent sovereignty of the Kiowa Tribe is recognized by the United States through treaties, statutes, executive orders, and judicial decisions, affirming the Kiowa Tribe's exclusive right to self-governance, including authority over its data and information; and

WHEREAS, the digital age presents both opportunities and threats to the sovereignty of Native Nations, as external corporations, government agencies, and other entities seek to collect, analyze, and monetize tribal data without the free, prior, and informed consent of the Tribe; and

WHEREAS, the Kiowa Tribe asserts its sovereign right to regulate, protect, and control all data concerning its citizens, governance, lands, cultural heritage, and economic enterprises, ensuring that such data is not exploited, misused, or otherwise accessed without tribal authorization; and

WHEREAS, unauthorized data collection, surveillance, and artificial intelligence applications using tribal data pose significant risks to the privacy, security, and autonomy of the Tribe and its citizens; and

WHEREAS, the Legislature of the Kiowa Tribe has determined that formal tribal laws and regulatory measures are necessary to establish strong protections against unauthorized access, collection, storage, and use of tribal data;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the Kiowa Tribe formally affirms its inherent right to Tribal Data Sovereignty, declaring that all data concerning the Tribe, its citizens, lands, governance, and cultural heritage is the exclusive property of the Kiowa Tribe and subject only to its jurisdiction;

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that any collection, access, use, storage, sharing, or transfer of tribal data without the explicit, written consent of the Kiowa Tribe Legislature is prohibited and shall be subject to enforcement under tribal law;

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Kiowa Tribe Legislature shall establish or designate an appropriate tribal agency to oversee, regulate, and enforce policies governing data privacy and sovereignty, ensuring compliance with this resolution;

BE IT FINALLY RESOLVED, that this resolution shall take immediate effect upon adoption.

C E R T I F I C A T I O N

The foregoing resolution KL-CY-2025-005, was duly voted upon by the Legislature on April 12, 2025, at a Legislative Regular Session XCVI-(96) with a vote of (6) in favor and (0) opposed, (0) abstaining, and (1) absent, pursuant to the authority vested in the Legislature by the Constitution of the Kiowa Tribe.

Michael Primus

Speaker of the Legislature

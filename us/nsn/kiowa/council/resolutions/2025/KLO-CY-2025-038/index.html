KLO-CY-2025-038 - Untitled [Overrides Veto of KL-CY-2025-026]

SUBJECT: To Override A Veto Issued by the Chairman of a Resolution/Bill KL-CY-2025-026 First Amendment to the Audit Authorization, Acceptance, and Responsibility Act.

WHEREAS, Article VI, Section 6(c) and (d) of the Tribe's Constitution grants the Legislature the authority to override a Chairman's veto of a Bill or budget by passing a Legislative Order; and,

WHEREAS, On October 11, 2025, the Legislature passed a Bill entitled, "The Nomination and Confirmation of Appointees Act of 2025." (Resolution KL-CY-2025-026); and,

WHEREAS, On October 30th, 2025, the Chairman of the Tribe vetoed Resolution KL-CY-2025-022 citing, "reasons for veto by Chairman (see attached statement)"; and,

NOW THEREFORE IT BE RESOLVED, that the Legislature hereby overrides the Chairman's veto of Resolution KL-CY-2025-026 The Nomination and Confirmation of Appointees Act of 2025.

C E R T I F I C A T I O N

The foregoing Legislative Override Order KLO-CY-2025-038 was duly voted and approved upon by the Legislature on November 14, 2025, at a Legislative Order Session XIX-(19) with a vote of (6) in favor and (0) opposed, (0) abstaining, and (1) absent, pursuant to the authority vested in the Legislature by the Constitution of the Kiowa Tribe.

Michael Primus

Speaker of the Legislature

KL-CY-2025-024 - A Resolution of the Kiowa Tribe Submission of a 477 Plan for Self-Determination.

WHEREAS, - the Kiowa Tribe is a federally recognized Indian tribe, organized under the Indian Reorganization Act of 1934 and operating under the Kiowa Tribe's Revised Constitution and Bylaws; and

WHEREAS, the Kiowa Tribe exercises its inherent sovereign authority through its lawful governing body, which is empowered through its inherent authority, to take actions necessary to protect the sustainability and autonomy of the Kiowa Tribe and the well-being, education, and development of its citizens; and

WHEREAS, Public Law 102-477, known as the Indian Employment, Training and Related Services Consolidation Act, authorizes a coordination of federal funding for employment, training, education, and related support services into a single, coordinated program; and

WHEREAS, such consolidation under Public Law 102-477 will allow the Kiowa Tribe to exercise greater efficiency, educational sovereignty, and self-determination in the design and implementation of programs and services for its people; and

WHEREAS, The Kiowa Tribe is actively pursuing greater self-determination, including participation in Public Law 102-477 as part of that effort, and any other applicable approved federal programs that advance Kiowa tribal interests; and

WHEREAS, the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA) Grant, administered through the Career Development Program within the Education Division of the Kiowa Tribe—also referred to as the Kiowa Education Agency—is a required component for consolidation under Public Law 102-477; and

WHEREAS, the Kiowa Education Agency has actively engaged in collaborative planning efforts, seeking guidance from other 477 Tribes, the U.S. Department of the Interior, and key program stakeholders identified for integration;

NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, that the Kiowa Tribe hereby grants authorization for the integration of eligible federal funding and associated programs, at its discretion, from any applicable federal agencies. These may include, but are not limited to, the Departments of Education, Labor, the Interior, Office of Child Care and the Child Care Development Fund (CCDF), Energy, Agriculture, Commerce, Transportation, Homeland Security, Housing and Urban Development, Justice, and/or Veterans Affairs into a unique Kiowa 477 Plan, as governed by Public Law 102-477; and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Kiowa Tribe hereby approves the development and submission of the K.E.A.'s formal 477 plan to the U.S. Department of the Interior for administration of its 477 Program, in accordance with applicable law and federal guidelines;

BE IT FURTHER AND FINALLY RESOLVED, that this resolution approving the Tribe's 477 Plan and Program shall remain in full force and effect unless and until it is formally rescinded by the governing body of the Kiowa Tribe.

C E R T I F I C A T I O N

The foregoing Resolution KL-CY-2025-024 was duly voted upon by the Legislature on October 11, 2025, at a Legislative Regular Session CII-(102), with a vote of (6) in favor and (0) opposed, (0) abstaining, and (1) absent, according to the authority vested in the Legislature by the Constitution of the Kiowa Tribe.

Michael Primus

Speaker of the Legislature
